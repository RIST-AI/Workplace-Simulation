Workplace Supervision Assessment

This interactive assessment will test your ability to maintain WHS processes and supervise staff performance in a simulated agricultural workplace environment.

Assessment Overview:

  • You will be assigned the role of supervisor for a small agricultural team (4-5 people)
  • The simulation will take place over 2 hours
  • You will need to complete 4 main activities
  • Your responses and decisions will be recorded for assessment

Simulation Activities:

  1. WHS Meeting: Plan and conduct a WHS consultation meeting
  2. Hazard Identification: Identify hazards and complete risk assessments
  3. Emergency Response: Respond to a simulated emergency
  4. Staff Supervision: Create work plans and address performance issues
